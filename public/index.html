Funding

The fund raisers help to offset out of pocket costs from each of us. The proceeds we raise from the fund raisers will be combined with our $250 per family commitment to pay for the reunion expenses, i.e., banquet, T-shirts, welcoming bags, dinner Friday or Saturday or both days, etc.

Regarding the $250 per household, it is of the utmost importance that each household please be vigilant in paying. If you require a payment plan, we ask that you please pay a minimum of $25 per month until paid in full.

Deadline for paying the $250 per household is June 1, 2018.

Banquet

Another topic we would like to begin addressing is the dress code for the banquet. The banquet is a wonderful gathering for our family to bring our reunion to a close. We want everyone of course, to be comfortable in mind and appearance. So, with respect, we are asking everyone to dress in semi-formal attire for the banquet. That translates to attire that you would not wear to a picnic or a ball game, but rather attire that is appropriate for an evening dinner (men: suit or dress slacks, dress shirt and tie / women: dress or dressy suit). The dress code for the banquet will continue to be firmly emphasized. We want everyone's participation, but even more so, your cooperation. Thank you.
